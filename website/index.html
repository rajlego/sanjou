What is the Block Method?

The Block Method is a productivity system from David Cain's "How to Do Things" guide. It's based on the idea that focused, uninterrupted work in 25-minute blocks is more effective than trying to work continuously.

Sanjou ("appearance" in Japanese) helps you implement this method with a desktop app that tracks your blocks, manages tasks, and keeps you accountable with a pre-block checklist.
